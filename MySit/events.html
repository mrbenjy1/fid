Events and Hours

"Pawsitive Brews & Bites" Adoption Event:

Partnering with local breweries and restaurants for an afternoon event. Attendees can enjoy craft beverages and delicious food while meeting adoptable dogs in a relaxed and social atmosphere.

Date & Time: May 3, 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM

"Saturday Morning Snuggles" Volunteer Meet & Greet with Puppies:

Focus on recruiting new volunteers by offering a heartwarming experience with the shelter's youngest residents.

Date & Time: May 10, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

"Strut Your Mutt" Community Dog Walk & Fun Day:

A family-friendly dog walk through at the local park, followed by a fun-filled day at a designated outdoor space.

Date & Time: May 18, 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM (Walk starting around 4:30 PM)
